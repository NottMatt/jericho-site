Artbook | Matt Tiemersma

All work presented here is my own work, but I do not own rights to the subject of all pieces.

I believe in the right to appropriate the work of other artists, as long as transformation or education occurs.

Feel free to use my work, credited or not, under these principles.

Trios Dark

This piece was an homage to our Apex Legends squad, and inspired by acrylic laser-cut pieces we had made previously.

Blender, 3D render, acrylic, abstract, dark mode

Trios Light

This piece was created as a light-mode rendition of the first Trios piece. It is a bit simpler, with less lighting effects and a more flat appear.

Duos - Octane and Valkyrie

This piece is an upgrade on the Trios set, using the assets from the Apex Legends game files. It portrays Octane and Valkyrie in legendary skins doing some classic poses.

Blender, 3D render, rigging, in-game assets, edge-lighting

Octane Jumppad
